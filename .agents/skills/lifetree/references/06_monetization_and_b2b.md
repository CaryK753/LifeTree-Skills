# Commercial Business & Monetization Architecture

## 1. Monetization Matrix & Tiering

| Tier | Price | Topic Limit | Key Capabilities |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Free Tier** | $0 | 1 Topic | Official standard connectors, basic path visualization. |
| **Pro Tier** | $19–$29 / mo | Up to 3 Topics | Cross-topic ripple effect calculation, community plugins, hourly real-time monitoring, high-risk circuit-breaker alerts, fixed monthly export quota of deep reports. |
| **Life OS Tier** | $69–$99 / mo | Unlimited | Unlimited topics, top-tier LLM trend prediction, unlimited deep research exports, priority compute pipeline. |
| **B2B SaaS Tier** | $299–$599 / seat / mo | Unlimited | Designed for immigration law firms, study abroad agencies, family offices. Interactive sales tool & client CRM risk dashboard. |

## 2. Developer Revenue Sharing Mechanics
- 20% of net consumer subscription revenues allocated to third-party open-source data plugin developers.
- Payouts computed automatically based on verified usage metrics in GraphRAG queries.
